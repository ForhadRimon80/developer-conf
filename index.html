Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before attending the conference.

What is included with my ticket?

Your ticket includes access to conference sessions, workshops, networking events, lunch, coffee breaks, and digital session recordings.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes. All keynote sessions will be recorded and made available to attendees after the conference.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Please contact our support team for assistance.

Do I need to bring my laptop?

We recommend bringing a laptop for hands-on workshops, but it is not required for keynote sessions.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. Every attendee will receive a digital certificate after the conference.
